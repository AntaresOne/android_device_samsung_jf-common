Softkeys manager is a shell script to enable or disable leds/haptic feedback of back & menu softkeys.
Can be launched from terminal and features user-choice options plus the ability to automatically recognize if leds (or haptic feedback if option 2 is choosen) are on or off.
All that you must do is to choose option 1 or 2, the magic is automatically done.
